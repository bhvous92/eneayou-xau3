7w6

Individuals with the 7w6 wing combination balance enthusiasm with a need for security. They combine the adventurous and enthusiastic nature of Type 7 with the loyalty and cautiousness of Type 6. They seek new experiences while valuing stability, often displaying a lively and curious demeanor tempered by a consideration for safety and loyalty.


Marked by assertiveness and a thirst for adventure, 7w8 individuals blend the enthusiasm of Type 7 with the assertiveness and independence of Type 8. They are bold, seeking new experiences and adventures while asserting their independence and control over situations, embodying both a zest for life and a desire for autonomy.
