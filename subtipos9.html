TYPE 09 SUBTYPES

9w8

9w1

Individuals with the 9w8 wing blend peace and assertiveness. They combine the peace-loving nature of Type 9 with the assertiveness and independence of Type 8. They seek harmony and accommodation while also valuing their independence and asserting themselves when needed, embodying both a desire for peace and a strength of character.


Marked by a pursuit of harmony and perfection, 9w1 individuals balance the peace-loving nature of Type 9 with the perfectionism and idealism of Type 1. They seek inner and outer peace while striving for personal and moral perfection, often embodying a quiet idealism and a desire for harmony and perfection in themselves and their surroundings.
