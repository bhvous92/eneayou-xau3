TYPE 01 SUBTYPES

1w9

1w2

Individuals with the 1w9 wing exhibit a blend of principled perfectionism and a contemplative nature. They prioritize inner harmony, striving for moral impeccability and adherence to standards. With a quieter, more introverted demeanor, they seek to perfect their ideals with a calm, reflective approach. This wing combination often embodies a balanced pursuit of justice and a desire for tranquility, aiming to harmonize personal integrity with a peaceful, principled existence.


Those with the 1w2 wing are dedicated to perfectionism coupled with a service-oriented mindset. They possess a strong moral compass and a drive to better society. Balancing their quest for personal ideals with a compassionate and supportive nature towards others, these individuals strive for both personal and communal improvement. They find fulfillment in serving others while maintaining high ethical standards, embodying a blend of principled idealism and empathetic assistance to create positive change.
