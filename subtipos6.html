TYPE 06 SUBTYPES

6w5

6w7

Embodying the independent and analytical traits of Type 6 with the introspective nature of Type 5, 6w5 individuals are cautious and self-reliant thinkers. They focus on both security and intellectual understanding, preferring to rely on their analytical capabilities while maintaining a more reserved and self-reliant approach.


Combining the cautiousness of Type 6 with the adventurous and sociable tendencies of Type 7, 6w7 individuals are vigilant yet outgoing. They balance their need for security with a desire for excitement and variety, often seeking to explore new experiences while maintaining a cautious approach.
