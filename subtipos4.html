TYPE 04 SUBTYPES

4w3

4w5

Encompassing the expressive nature of Type 4 with the achievement-focused attitude of Type 3, 4w3 individuals seek recognition and success while staying true to their unique, authentic selves. They are driven to express their individuality creatively while striving for acknowledgment and accomplishment in their endeavors.


Combining the introspective and individualistic traits of Type 4 with the analytical and reserved nature of Type 5, 4w5 individuals are creative and independent thinkers. They prioritize introspection, exploring their unique identities deeply while maintaining a more withdrawn and observant demeanor.
