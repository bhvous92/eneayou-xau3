TYPE 03 SUBTYPES

3w2

3w4

Combining the ambition of Type 3 with the supportive nature of Type 2, 3w2s are enthusiastic achievers with a strong focus on assisting others. They excel in social situations, striving for success while maintaining a supportive and affable demeanor. Driven by recognition and admiration, they channel their ambition into helping others, fostering an environment of success and supportiveness


Individuals with the 3w4 wing blend the success-oriented mindset of Type 3 with the individualistic nature of Type 4. They prioritize personal authenticity and introspection while pursuing success. More reserved and introspective than other Type 3 combinations, they seek recognition while staying true to their unique, introspective identities, balancing achievement with a deeper sense of self-awareness.
