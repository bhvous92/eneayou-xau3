TYPE 08 SUBTYPES

8w7

8w9

A fusion of assertiveness and adventurousness, 8w7 individuals are assertive and independent thinkers who seek excitement and new experiences. They combine the assertiveness and strength of Type 8 with the enthusiasm and openness to new adventures of Type 7. They are bold and assertive, driven by a desire for both power and thrilling experiences.


Those with the 8w9 wing prioritize peace and stability while still maintaining assertiveness when necessary. They combine the strength and assertiveness of Type 8 with the calm and accommodating nature of Type 9. They are often patient and accommodating, valuing harmony and stability while being assertive when their principles are at stake.
