TYPE 05 SUBTYPES

5w4

5w6

With a blend of intellectual depth from Type 5 and a focus on individuality and creativity from Type 4, 5w4 individuals are introspective and creative thinkers. They emphasize unique self-expression, often seeking knowledge and introspection to further their creative pursuits and understanding of themselves.


Balancing the intellectual curiosity of Type 5 with the cautious and security-oriented mindset of Type 6, 5w6 individuals are analytical and cautious thinkers. They prioritize security while seeking knowledge and reliability, often applying their insights and analytical skills to maintain a sense of safety and understanding in their surroundings.
