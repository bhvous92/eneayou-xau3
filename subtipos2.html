OS SUBTIPOS DE 02

2w1

2w3

Characterized by empathetic support and ethical considerations, the 2w1 wing combines the nurturing tendencies of Type 2 with the principled perfectionism of Type 1. These individuals prioritize aiding others while maintaining a strong moral compass and personal integrity. They offer assistance in alignment with their ethical standards, often fostering a supportive environment that embodies both empathy and a sense of righteousness.


With an inclination towards ambition and social adeptness, 2w3 individuals blend the helpfulness of Type 2 with the achievement-oriented nature of Type 3. These individuals are driven by a desire to succeed while providing assistance and support. They navigate social situations adeptly, seeking recognition and accomplishment in their efforts to help others. This combination embodies a charismatic and achievement-focused approach to aiding those around them.
